🗺️ Preparation Strategy (6-Month Plan)

Phase 1

Month 1–2: Foundation Building

  • Complete Units 1, 4, 5 (historical, biological, and basic cognitive)
  • Read Morgan & King for core concepts
  • Make unit-wise summary notes
  • Attempt 20 PYQs per unit after completion
Phase 2

Month 3–4: High-Weightage Units

  • Focus on Units 2, 3, 7 (high scoring areas)
  • Master research designs and statistical tests
  • Learn all major personality theories with theorists
  • Take 1 unit-wise mock test weekly
Phase 3

Month 5: Complete Coverage

  • Cover Units 6, 8, 9, 10
  • Solve all PYQs from 2018–2024
  • Identify and revisit weak areas
  • Take 2 full mock tests per week
Phase 4

Month 6: Revision & Mock Tests

  • Intensive revision of all summary notes
  • Solve 1 full mock test daily (last 2 weeks)
  • Focus on Paper I (teaching aptitude) practice
  • Review answer sheets and improve accuracy
